# Software Engineer

**Location:** Bangalore, India · **In-office** · Full-time
**Experience:** 2–4 years

## About the Role

We're looking for a Software Engineer to build and own features end-to-end as part of a product engineering team. You'll write production code, ship to real users, and grow your craft alongside senior engineers who care about quality. This is a hands-on role for someone early in their career who takes ownership beyond their assigned tasks and is excited to work in an AI-forward engineering culture.

## What You'll Do

* Design, build, test, and ship backend services and APIs that power our products.
* Take ownership of features from problem definition through deployment and monitoring.
* Write clean, well-tested, maintainable code and participate in code reviews.
* Debug and resolve issues across the stack, including in production.
* Collaborate with product, design, and fellow engineers to deliver impact.
* Use AI coding tools effectively as part of a modern development workflow.

## What We're Looking For

* 2–4 years of experience as a software engineer, ideally in a product engineering organization.
* Strong coding fundamentals — data structures & algorithms, OOP, databases, operating systems, and networking basics.
* Hands-on experience with backend/API development and at least one cloud platform (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
* Exposure to distributed systems concepts.
* A track record of impact-oriented projects and ownership beyond assigned tasks.
* Clear communication and a collaborative, feedback-receptive working style.
* A degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related engineering field is preferred but not required — demonstrable product impact carries equal weight.

## Nice to Have

* Experience with LLMs, GenAI, AI agents, or agentic workflows.

## Our Interview Process

A 3-round process (each round ~60 minutes):

1. **AI-Enabled Coding & Problem Solving** — a live coding problem where AI tools are explicitly allowed; we evaluate how you break down problems and work with AI, not memorized algorithms.
2. **PR Review & Code Comprehension** — review a realistic pull request for bugs, design, and tests.
3. **Hiring Manager — Technical Taste & Fit** — design fundamentals, engineering judgment, and team fit.
