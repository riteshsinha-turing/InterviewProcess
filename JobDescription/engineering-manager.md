# Engineering Manager

**Location:** Bangalore, India · **In-office** · Full-time
**Experience:** 8–12 years total, including 3+ years in engineering leadership

## About the Role

We're hiring an Engineering Manager to lead and grow a team of engineers while staying close to the technology. You'll own your team's delivery, health, and growth — hiring and developing engineers, driving execution, and partnering across functions — without losing the technical judgment that earns your team's trust. This is a people-leadership role for someone who has built and run high-performing teams and brings a strong engineering foundation to the job.

## What You'll Do

* Own your team's delivery, health, and growth — planning, execution, and shipping across one or more teams.
* Hire, grow, and retain engineers; run 1:1s, performance management, and career development.
* Set and raise engineering standards, processes, and ways of working.
* Provide technical direction and pressure-test decisions without micromanaging.
* Partner with product, design, and leadership; manage up and across.
* Own team-level operational excellence, including incident and on-call ownership.

## What We're Looking For

* **8–12 years of total engineering experience, including 3+ years of direct engineering leadership** (people management) — owning a team's delivery, growth, and performance, not solely tech-lead/IC seniority.
* A track record of hiring, growing, and retaining engineers, with positive retention and team-health outcomes.
* Delivery ownership across one or more teams and strong cross-functional partnership with PM, design, and leadership.
* A strong underlying engineering foundation comparable to a Staff Engineer: driving architecture and technical direction across multiple services or teams.
* Deep distributed systems and cloud-native architecture knowledge; engineering judgment retained alongside the management role.
* Demonstrated impact on multi-team technical direction, migrations/modernization, reliability/performance, and engineering standards.
* A degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related engineering field is preferred but not required — we weight work outcomes most heavily.

## Nice to Have

* AI platform architecture experience.
* Agentic systems / LLM infrastructure experience.

## Our Interview Process

A 4-round process (each round ~60 minutes):

1. **PR Review & Code Comprehension** — review a realistic pull request to confirm hands-on engineering judgment.
2. **System Design — Distributed Systems** — architectural judgment and operational awareness expected of a manager.
3. **Engineering Leadership** — people management, hiring and team building, delivery and execution, cross-functional partnership, and conflict resolution.
4. **Hiring Manager — Technical Taste & Fit** — leadership scope, org fit, and technical taste.
