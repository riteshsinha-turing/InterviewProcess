# Senior Software Engineer

**Location:** Bangalore, India · **In-office** · Full-time
**Experience:** 6–8 years

## About the Role

We're hiring a Senior Software Engineer to own significant pieces of our product and platform end-to-end. You'll lead the design and delivery of complex systems, raise the engineering bar through reviews and mentorship, and partner across functions to ship reliable software at scale. This is a high-ownership role for an engineer who combines strong distributed-systems depth with sound design judgment and an AI-forward mindset.

## What You'll Do

* Own end-to-end delivery of features and services — from design through production operation.
* Design scalable, reliable distributed systems and drive key technical decisions.
* Debug and resolve production issues; improve reliability and performance.
* Raise code quality and design standards through thorough, constructive reviews.
* Mentor junior engineers and contribute to a strong engineering culture.
* Collaborate cross-functionally with product, design, and other teams.

## What We're Looking For

* 6–8 years of software engineering experience, ideally at scalable product companies.
* Proven ownership: end-to-end delivery, production debugging, and mentoring more junior engineers.
* Strong distributed systems and cloud engineering skills (AWS, GCP, or Azure).
* Solid code quality, testing, and design practices.
* A track record with measurable scale, reliability improvements, and architecture participation.
* Strong cross-functional collaboration and clear communication.
* A degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related engineering field is preferred but not required — we weight work outcomes most heavily.

## Nice to Have

* Microservices / platform engineering experience.
* AI/LLM platform integrations.

## Our Interview Process

A 4-round process (each round ~60 minutes):

1. **AI-Enabled Coding & Problem Solving** — a live coding problem where AI tools are explicitly allowed; we evaluate problem decomposition and how you work with AI.
2. **PR Review & Code Comprehension** — architecture-aware review of a realistic pull request, including security and scale considerations.
3. **System Design — Distributed Systems** — how systems are designed, fail, operate, and scale in production.
4. **Hiring Manager — Technical Taste & Fit** — system design, engineering judgment, ownership, and team & culture fit.
