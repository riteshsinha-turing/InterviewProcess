# SDET — Software Development Engineer in Test

**Location:** Bangalore, India · **In-office** · Hybrid
**Experience:** 5+ years across software development and testing

## About the Role

We're hiring an SDET (Software Development Engineer in Test) to own test automation across our frontend and backend, and to make quality a first-class part of how we ship. You'll write and maintain robust automated test frameworks, build the CI pipelines that run them, and partner with engineers to keep our releases fast and reliable. This is a hands-on engineering role for someone who codes as well as they test and who treats flaky, slow, or low-signal tests as bugs to be fixed.

## What You'll Do

* Design, write, and maintain automated tests and test frameworks for frontend and backend.
* Build and own cloud test pipelines / CI (e.g. GitHub Actions) — parallelization, reporting, and merge gating.
* Keep the suite trustworthy: detect and eliminate flakiness, speed up runs, and improve signal.
* Define test strategy — what to automate, at which level, and where to shift testing left.
* Partner with developers to improve testability and own release and regression quality.
* Debug failing tests and pipelines and drive issues to resolution.

## What We're Looking For

* **5+ years across software development and testing**, with a strong automation focus.
* Solid coding fundamentals — you write and maintain test code and frameworks, not just record-and-playback scripts.
* Hands-on experience with modern automated testing frameworks across frontend and backend (e.g. **Playwright, Selenium, Cypress**, and API/integration testing tooling).
* Experience building and maintaining **cloud test pipelines / CI** and integrating automated suites into CI/CD (e.g. **GitHub Actions**).
* A track record of test coverage and quality improvements, flaky-test reduction, and framework/tooling ownership.
* Clear communication and a collaborative, quality-first working style.
* Formal academic qualifications are **not** a gating requirement for this role — we weight hands-on development and test-automation experience most heavily.

## Nice to Have

* AI-assisted test generation and agentic/automated testing workflows.
