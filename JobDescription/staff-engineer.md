# Staff Engineer

**Location:** Bangalore, India · **In-office** · Full-time
**Experience:** 8–12 years

## About the Role

We're looking for a Staff Engineer to set technical direction across multiple services and teams. You'll be a force multiplier — driving architecture, raising engineering standards, leading complex cross-team initiatives, and mentoring senior engineers. This is a senior IC role for a deeply technical engineer who shapes how the organization builds software and whose design judgment others adopt.

## What You'll Do

* Drive architecture and technical direction across multiple services and teams.
* Lead complex, cross-cutting initiatives — migrations, modernization, and platform/system evolution.
* Own reliability and performance at scale; make high-leverage engineering decisions and tradeoffs.
* Raise engineering standards through design docs, standards, and reviews that others adopt.
* Mentor senior engineers and influence technical strategy beyond a single team.
* Partner with engineering leadership and cross-functional stakeholders on technical roadmaps.

## What We're Looking For

* 8–12 years of software engineering experience with a track record of technical leadership.
* Experience driving architecture and technical direction across multiple services or teams.
* Cross-team influence: platform/system evolution and raising engineering standards.
* Deep distributed systems and cloud-native architecture expertise; strong engineering judgment.
* Demonstrated impact on multi-team technical direction, migrations/modernization, and reliability/performance.
* A history of mentoring senior engineers and producing standards and design docs that others adopt.
* A degree in Computer Science, IT, or a related engineering field is preferred but not required — we weight work outcomes most heavily.

## Nice to Have

* AI platform architecture experience.
* Agentic systems / LLM infrastructure experience.

## Our Interview Process

A 4-round process (each round ~60 minutes):

1. **AI-Enabled Coding & Problem Solving** — a challenging live coding problem where AI tools are explicitly allowed; we evaluate how intentionally you drive an AI workflow and refactor generated code to production quality.
2. **PR Review & Code Comprehension** — review centered on standards, scale, and rollout risk.
3. **System Design — Distributed Systems** — deep cross-service tradeoffs and operational excellence at scale.
4. **Hiring Manager — Technical Taste & Fit** — system design, cross-team technical direction, technical taste, and team & culture fit.
